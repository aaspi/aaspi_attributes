Mass Transport Deposits

Geologic Setting

Mass transport deposits form in deep marine environments where slope failure has occured causing mass transports of sediment to travel basinward. Mass transport deposits contain information on the kinematic structure of the slope failure and the travel amount. When slope failure occurs, material is travels downslope above the basal shear surface which develops due to progressive shear failure. In the shear zone where mass transport complex travel over there is more often than not erosion of the underlying sediments.

Bull S., J. Cart and M. Huuse, 2009, A review of kinematic indicators from mass-transport complexes using 3D seismic data:Marine and Petroleum geology,26,1132-1151
