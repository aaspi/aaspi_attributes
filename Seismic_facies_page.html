AASPI Seismic Facies Expression Overview
Summary
Seismic Amplitude Expression Seismic facies expression of geologic features defines the reflector configuration or pattern associated with that particular feature. Seismic facies are 3 dimensional deposits and structures. As such, the features associated with seismic facies are also 3 dimensional and are interpreted on both 3D and 2D seismic data. To define the seismic expression of geologic features takes large amounts of background research and practice. These web pages are meant to streamline some of the process for users.
Seismic Attribute Expression Seismic attribute expression of facies dives into the different applications of seismic attributes. These expressions are dependent on the attribute and the geologic detail of interest. Attributes are common tools interpreters use to verify and to create a clearer image of seismic facies.
