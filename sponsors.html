Geophysical Insights Geophysical Insights develops and applies new disruptive technologies to dramatically reduce exploration risks, field development costs, and the time required for interpretation. Paradise®, an off-the-shelf, multi-attribute seismic analysis software platform by Geophysical Insights, is the latest of the company’s developments. Launched commercially in late 2013, Paradise enables interpreters to easily apply advanced pattern recognition capabilities on multiple attributes through guided workflows, including Self-Organizing Maps (SOMs) and Principal Component Analysis (PCA).

Key Lab of China University of Petroleum Being the core business of oil and gas exploration and development, petroleum and natural gas engineering is a knowledge, technology and capital intensive project centering on petroleum and natural gas exploitation and transportation. Based on the national key discipline of petroleum and natural gas engineering, MOE Key Laboratory of Petroleum Engineering set up 5 main research directions, which are oil and gas drilling and exploration mechanics and control engineering, wellbore multiphase flow theory and technology, oil and gas reservoir porous flow theory and development engineering, oil field chemistry and enhanced oil recovery technology and oil and gas pipage and storage technology. With a long history of combining production, study and research, this lab has accomplished considerable technology development and promotion, training and conculting in projects of western oil and gas exploration and development, offshore well drilling and completion, oil recovery enhancement, hyposmosis oil reservoir high-efficient development and oil and gas pipage. More than fifty percent research outcomes could be transferred into practical productive forces, which contribute to the innovative development and industrialization of petroleum and natural gas engineering.
